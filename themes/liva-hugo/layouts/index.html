Our Goal

Ventilator design is a highly complicated and niche field involving many engineering disciplines, medical knowledge and an understanding of rules and regulations. While many of the ongoing fast-turn attempts to build an emergency device are truly admirable, it is likely that most will either never make it to volume production, or prove too hazardous and unreliable for practical use. Two of the RED founders, Edward Ayrapetian and Mohan Gurunathan, have over 5 years of previous design experience working on medical ventilators. As engineers with a solid understanding of medical ventilator design, they are uniquely positioned to develop a fast-track ventilator solution which can be rapidly scaled up to help meet the needs of the global population.
